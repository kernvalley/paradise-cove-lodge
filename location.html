Location

We are located on Highway 178 on the south side of Lake Isabella, just 3 miles east of the dam. Only one hour from Bakersfield or Ridgecrest, and 3 hours from Los Angeles; a perfect place for a weekend get away.​

Have dinner and stay for the night.​

Come and stay for the weekend, or longer. There is so much to do in Lake Isabella: fishing and water sports including waterskiing or wakeboarding and jet skis, biking and hiking the many trails in our area.

We are in a great location for an overnight stop for those traveling to Las Vegas, Death Valley, the coast or Sequoia National Forest.

Stay in one of our comfortable rooms and have dinner with great views of the lake and mountains.
